01 — Features

Everything you need to track lending

Built for real people who lend money to friends, family and colleagues. No bloat. Just what works.

F.01

Track people

Add by name and phone. Full transaction history per person. Works for anyone you lend to.

F.02

Log in 10 seconds

Amount, date, cash or UPI, optional note. Tap save. No friction whatsoever.

F.03

Smart reminders

Auto-remind after N days. Set promise dates. Send a pre-filled WhatsApp in one tap.

F.04

Lent + Borrowed

Track both what others owe you AND what you owe others. Complete financial picture.

F.05

Private by design

SQLite on your device. No ads. No analytics. Optional Google backup. Biometric lock.

F.06

Stats & insights

Longest pending, monthly totals, average return time. Know exactly where you stand.

02 — How it works

Four steps. Under 10 seconds.

No learning curve. Open the app, do the thing, close it.

STEP 01

Add a person

Search existing or add someone new with their name and phone number.

STEP 02

Log the amount

Enter amount, pick cash or UPI, add a note if you want. Save.

STEP 03

Get reminded

App reminds you after N days. Send WhatsApp message in one tap.

STEP 04

Record return

Money came back? Log it. Balance clears. Archive the person.

Why I built RinBook

I kept forgetting who I lent money to. Not the big amounts — you always remember those. It's the small ones. ₹200 here. ₹500 there. Three months later you genuinely can't remember if you asked for it back or not.

I looked for an app. Everything I found was either too complicated, full of ads, required creating an account before showing me anything, or felt like it was built for a finance team — not someone lending money to their cousin.

"So I spent a few months building exactly what I needed. Then I put it on the Play Store."

RinBook is offline first. Your data lives on your device in a local SQLite database. No account required to use it. Sign in with Google if you want backup and sync across devices — your data gets backed up to a private encrypted database that only you can access.

Free. No ads. No subscription. No premium tier. I built it because I needed it. Sharing it because maybe you do too.
