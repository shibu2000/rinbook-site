What it does

F.01
Track people
Add by name + phone. Works for friends, family, colleagues. Full transaction history per person.
F.02
Log fast
Amount, date, cash or UPI, note. 10 seconds. Done. No friction, no accounts required to start.
F.03
Smart reminders
Auto-remind after N days. Promise dates. One-tap WhatsApp message with pre-filled text.
F.04
Both directions
Lent mode + Borrowed mode. Track what others owe you AND what you owe others. Complete picture.
F.05
Offline first
SQLite on device. Works without internet. Sign in with Google for backup and cross-device sync.
F.06
Private
No ads. No tracking. No analytics. Data never sold. Optional biometric lock. Your data, period.
02 — the app

Designed for real use

No bloat. No onboarding questionnaires. No subscription prompts. Opens fast, shows your data, gets out of your way.

  • Home screen shows everyone + their balance at a glance
  • Tap a person → full transaction history
  • FAB button → add transaction in seconds
  • Reminders tab → who needs a follow-up today
  • Stats → monthly trends, longest pending, totals
  • Settings → currency, reminder days, dark mode, lock

Why I built this

I kept forgetting who I lent money to. Not the big amounts — those you remember. It's the small ones. ₹200 here, ₹500 there. Three months later you can't remember if you asked for it back or not. Awkward conversations. Forgotten debts. Lost friendships sometimes.

I looked for an app. Everything I found was either too complicated, full of ads, or felt like it was designed for a CFO, not someone lending money to their cousin.

"So I built something I'd actually use."

RinBook is offline-first — your data lives on your device, not my server. Sign in with Google if you want backup and sync. Otherwise, just use it. No account needed.

It's free. No ads. No in-app purchases. No premium tier. I built it to scratch my own itch, and I'm sharing it because maybe you have the same itch.
