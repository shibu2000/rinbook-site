What we collect

RinBook collects only what's needed to work:

  • Account info — if you sign in with Google, we get your name and email. that's all Google shares.
  • Transaction data — names, phone numbers, amounts, dates, notes you enter yourself.
  • App settings — currency preference, reminder days, dark mode. stored locally.

We do not collect location, device ID, usage analytics, or anything you didn't explicitly type in.

How we use it

  • Show you your lending history in the app
  • Send local push notifications for reminders
  • Sync and restore your data across devices (only if signed in)

Not used for ads, profiling, marketing, or anything else.

Where it lives

On your device: SQLite database. Works completely offline. You own it.

In the cloud (optional): If you sign in, data backs up to Supabase — encrypted, on AWS, tied to your Google account. Only you can access it via row-level security.

Auth tokens stored in Android Keystore / expo-secure-store.

Who sees it

Nobody. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with anyone.

Third-party services used:

  • Google Sign-In — auth only. Google's own privacy policy applies to that flow.
  • Supabase — encrypted database infrastructure. They cannot access your records.

How it's protected

  • All cloud traffic encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • Row Level Security — only your authenticated account can read/write your rows
  • Auth tokens in device secure enclave
  • Optional biometric/PIN lock in app settings

How long we keep it

As long as you use the app. You can delete anytime:

  • Delete a person → removes them + all their transactions from device + cloud
  • Settings → Delete account → wipes everything from Supabase permanently
  • Uninstall → removes all local data

Your rights

  • Access — all your data is visible in the app at all times
  • Delete — delete any record or your entire account
  • Export — email us and we'll send you everything
  • Correction — edit transactions within the 2-hour window

Children

RinBook is not intended for anyone under 13. We don't knowingly collect data from children. Contact us if you believe this has happened.

Changes

We'll update the date at the top if anything changes. Continued use = acceptance of updates. Nothing will ever change the core principle: your data is yours.
